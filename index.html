TDTCoin Tokenomics

TDT Token Distribution

The TDT token is designed to support the DeFi and Web3 ecosystem with a fixed total supply of 1 billion tokens. The distribution is optimized to ensure sustainability, community incentive, and long-term growth.

  • 40% - Ecosystem Development
  • 25% - Liquidity & Staking
  • 20% - Team & Advisors
  • 10% - IDO & Public Sale
  • 5% - Community & Airdrop Fund

A token burning mechanism will be implemented to reduce the total supply over time, increasing long-term value for TDT.
